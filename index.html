Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

What is ADITI?
ADITI is a premium, strategic geopolitics publication dedicated to deep, doctrine-first analysis of Indian defense, security, and foreign policy. We focus on long-form strategic clarity over daily news feeds.
How much does an article cost?
Individual premium dispatches are priced at ₹350 each. Once purchased, you retain lifetime access to the essay on any device.
Is there a subscription model?
Currently, ADITI is designed around single-article purchases, allowing readers to buy only the specific strategic analyses that interest them. An annual subscription model is planned for late 2026.
How often is ADITI published?
We publish six major print and digital editions a year. In between editions, standalone dispatches are released on an ongoing basis.
Who is ADITI for?
ADITI is written for policymakers, defense practitioners, military officers, strategic analysts, and citizens who want a rigorous, doctrine-level understanding of Indian national security.
